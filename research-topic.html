Project @: A Study of Community and Interpersonal Relationships in the Digital Age

We researched the overarching theme of physical vs. digital, with a focus on community. (Community: a network that provides sociability, information, support, and a sense of identity). 'Real world' interactions have changed drastically with the rise of digital, from social platforms and online dating to AI. For some, commenting and messaging are primary methods of communication with other humans. The difference between physical and digital community lies in the ability to customize ourselves and interactions with others.
