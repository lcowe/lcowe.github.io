@instant4color, A Screen-Print Series: I’m interested in the physical vs. digital, and the short-lived life of a post. I’ve recreated images from my personal Instagram feed through the silkscreen CMYK process. Printing images we’d typically only view on a screen through the four color process as an ode to time. Posts are generated so quickly and can feel so temporary, yet printmaking takes time and, like the internet, has permanent results.
