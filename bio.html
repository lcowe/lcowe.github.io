about us

Hi there,

We are an Ayr, Scotland based pop duo comprised of Xandra on vocals and John on keyboard and vocals, performing covers from the 50’s to current chart hits. We always play to the crowd, looking to keep everyone dancing the night away!

Although this current lineup has only been together since February this year we have many years of experience between us.

We’d love to work with you to set up some gigs and look forward to hearing from you.
